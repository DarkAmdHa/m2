What to expect from our Health & Safety Services

Find out what you can expect from the H&S team at Moorepay.

  • H&S Advice Line 24/7/365

    Our professional and qualified H&S advisors are only a phone call away any time you need support. Well provide advice thats tailored to your business and keep you compliant with legislation.

     

  • Dedicated account manager

    Theres no getting passed around from pillar to post, simply contact your dedicated account manager when you need them. Theyll get to know you and your business and become an extension of your own team.

  • Initial audit

    Our qualified team carry out an initial audit of your organisation to review existing policies, procedures, and documentation, and discuss your requirements.

  • Risk assessments

    Trust our specialists to carry out robust risk assessments and risk profiling across your business, so that youre meeting your legal obligations.

  • Accident reports and plans

    If an incident occurs, our specialists will undertake robust investigations, keep comprehensive paper trails, and provide action plans that mitigate future risk.

  • Job reviews and planning

    We provide method statements and create safe systems of work to identify job hazards and plan the safest way for your people to carry out their duties.

  • Customised H&S documents

    Youll get guidance notes, checklists and bespoke H&S policies tailored to your industry and business, along with advice on how to implement everything. All your documents will be updated regularly and securely stored in an online portal called the HR Hub.

  • Indemnity insurance

    Choose our insurance package for defending enforcement action relating to food, health or safety enforcement and appeals against an Improvement or Prohibition Notice by HSE, EHO and other statutory bodies.

  • Occupational Health

    Get access to a UK-wide network of clinicians to provide occupational health referrals via phone, or face-to-face. Get advice and guidance for next steps and access to occupational health referral reports.
